File naming convention for data collection


s#{l|r}#_{m|f}_{y|n}_{grn|blu|blk|red|ylw}_{observed reps}.txt

Example:
  
    Subject #1, Right Arm Set #2, Male, Exercises regularly, red band, 10 reps observed

        s1r2_m_y_red_10.txt

    Subject #2, Left Arm Set #1, Female, doesn't exercise regularly, blue band, 12 reps observed

        s2l1_f_n_blu_12.txt
